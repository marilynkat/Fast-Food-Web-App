Picking your next meal
Made Easy

Fast Food is a web application designed to make picking your next meal easy. Whether you're alone, with your friends, or your whole family, choosing your next meal shouldn't be a struggle.

Developers

How does Fast Food work?

Fast Food has three main functionalities. They include the food vote, random restaurant selector, and the bucketlist. Each functionality is meant to aid the user in choosing their next meal.

Food Vote

Our food vote page allows a group of people to vote on which restaurant to eat at next.

Random Selector

If you have no idea where to eat next, our random selector will choose a restaurant near you for you.

Bucketlist

Our bucketlist allows you to keep track of the restaurants you want to eat at and which you've been to already.
